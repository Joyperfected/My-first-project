HOW TO APPLY

Home > Admission > How to apply

We are now accepting applications for the 2023-2024 academic year. To register as a new user, please click on the START button. We offer admission on a rolling basis, therefore applications are processed as they are received, and grades are filled based on the school's intake needs. *Please note that positions are offered to students applying for grades 5 through 11. We do not offer positions for grade 12 only.

Due to the high volume of day applications, the applicant and his family must be currently living in our day boundary area and studying in the Ontario curriculum in order to qualify for a day position.

Please complete the online application and submit the $175 application fee by credit card. Once you have submitted the application you will have access to your parent portal where you will see your checklist. The following documents are required to be uploaded directly to the checklist:

  • Candidate statement form
  • Previous two years of final June report cards
  • Current year report cards (when available)
  • A sampling of certificates and awards
  • Student photo
  • Confidential School Recommendation Form: Email this form directly to your son's teacher using the form link found in your
  • Character skills snapshot(not available for grade 5 applicant)
Entrance Test for Grades 5 - 8

Applicants that have been studying in the Canadian curriculum for 3 years will write the CAT4 test at St. Andrew's College. Once the application has been submitted, please choose the test date link in your parent portal. If an applicant is unable to visit our campus to write the test, alternative arrangements can be made with the assigned Admission Officer.

All international applicants are required to write the SSAT. Register at ssat.org for the applicable option that is best suited for your son. Please ensure that the results are shared with St. Andrew's College, school code 6263. If SSAT is not available in specific geographic locations, alternate arrangements can be made with the assigned Admission Officer.

SSAT results will be accepted in place of the CAT4 if your son is applying to multiple schools.

Entrance Test for Grades 9 - 11

Applicants that have been studying in the Canadian curriculum for 3 years will write the CAT4 test. Test dates will be offered at St. Andrew's College. Once the application has been submitted, please choose the test date link in your parent portal. If an applicant is unable to visit our campus to write the test, alternative arrangements can be made with the assigned Admission Officer.

SSAT is required for international applicants. Register at ssat.org for the applicable option that is best suited for your son. Please ensure the results are shared with St. Andrew's College, school code 6263. If SSAT is not available in specific geographic locations, alternate arrangements can be made with the assigned Admission Officer.

It is the goal of St. Andrew's College to adhere to environmentally friendly initiatives. We request that all families complete the online version of our application. Should you encounter difficulties, please contact the Admission Office at 905-727-3178 ext. 303 for assistance.
